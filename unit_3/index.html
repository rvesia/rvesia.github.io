Tallahassee's premier venue for creative endeavors from beginners to pros!

Workshops

At Olive's Art Studio we offer a multitude of workshops. We serve students of all ages and backgrounds; children, adults, and seniors; beginners to pros; crafters to fine artists!
We have three art studios in our building, an outdoor garden, and are in walking distance to Fake Park. We utilize all of these spaces to offer lessons of all shapes and sizes! Workshops range from one-day-only events, reoccuring weekly or monthly workshops, and seasonal series workshops that can last 6-8 weeks!
At Olive's Art Studio you are sure to find your fit and begin exploring new materials and expressing your inner creativity!

Kids Course: Lost and Found Construction Bugs

In this 4 hour workshop, students will deconstruct lost and found items and turn them into 3-d bug sculptures. Two Olive's Studio instructors will lead this class, offering safety and cool ideas! Students will use found materials, wire, and colorful scraps to create works of art! Age ranges from 3rd - 6th grade. Class cost is $20 and includes all supplies (don't worry if you can't supply any junk, we have plenty)! Play time begins next saturday at noon and lasts until 4 p.m.

Book now while spots last!

Teacher Resources

Olive's Art Studio was founded by art educators who love our local teachers! Our instructors are available to come to your class and give presentations, share local crafts, and add a sense of community arts for you students! We also love to support educators and offer lesson plan packages, worksheets or handouts, classroom decor packs. Our education directors are even available for one-on-one consultations to help with curriculum planning, year-outlines, or individual lesson planning! Whether you are a local leon county teacher or a homeschool teacher, we can help! Check out our awesome Teacher Resources!
